Resources for Young Storytellers

Welcome to our collection of resources designed to help you unleash your creativity and become a fantastic storyteller!

Storytelling Techniques

  • Storytelling Techniques - Tips and techniques for telling captivating stories.
  • Story Prompts - Fun prompts to inspire your next story.

Writing Tools

  • Storybird - A platform for creating and sharing stories with illustrations.
  • Grammarly - A tool to help with spelling and grammar.

Illustration Resources

  • Pixabay - Free images and illustrations for your stories.
  • Drawing Tutorials - Learn how to illustrate your own stories.

Reading Resources

  • Audible Kids - A selection of audiobooks for children.
  • Storyline Online - Videos of actors reading children's books.

Interactive Apps

  • Toontastic - An app for creating animated stories.
  • Book Creator - An app for creating and publishing your own books.

Competitions and Challenges

  • KidLit Competitions - Information on writing competitions for children.
  • Writing Challenges - Participate in fun writing challenges.

Parent and Teacher Resources

  • Parenting Resources - Guides for parents to encourage storytelling.
  • Teaching Resources - Tools for educators to inspire young writers.

Community and Support

  • Storytelling Clubs - Find local clubs to share your stories.
  • Kids Writing Forums - Join discussions and share your work.
